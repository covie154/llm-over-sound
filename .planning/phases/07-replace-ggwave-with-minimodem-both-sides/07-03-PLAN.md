<objective>
Repoint the AHK frontend transport from ggwave to minimodem: load `minimodem_simple.dll`, pass `BAUD_RATE` to init, add the `Crc32Str` helper (matching the proven cross-language vector), and convert the send/receive path to single-frame + `crc` field with full-message retransmit on mismatch. Every layer above transport (GUI, msgid, compression LZNT1/Base62) is preserved.

Purpose: Per CONTEXT.md locked decisions, AHK keeps its `DllCall` model because `minimodem_simple.dll` mirrors `ggwave_simple.h`; only the namespace, the baud parameter, the new `crc` field, and single-frame framing change. CLAUDE.md: any framing/encoding change on one side MUST be mirrored on the other — Plan 04 mirrors this on Python. Split/reassemble stays dormant for v2 chunking.
Output: An AHK frontend that transmits/receives minimodem FSK single frames with CRC32 integrity.
</objective>
