- Check that `"Guidance"` does NOT appear in output
- Check that `NOT_DOCUMENTED` appears/doesn't appear as expected
- For structured tests: check colon-separated lines like `"Liver: See below"`
- For measurement tests: check that `"14.2"` appears where `{{measurement:liver_span_cm}}` was
- For group tests: check that `"The spleen, adrenal glands and pancreas are unremarkable."` appears (joint_normal) when all members unreported and interpolate=true
