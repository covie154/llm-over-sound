## Guidance

Normal kidney length: 9–12 cm (mild asymmetry up to 1.5 cm acceptable).

Bosniak classification does not apply to greyscale US. Instead, use "Bosniak x equivalent":
- Simple Cyst: Bosniak 1 equivalent, no follow-up is required.
- Cyst with few and thin septation: Bosniak 2 equivalent, no follow-up is required.
- Complex cyst: CT or CEUS Kidney is required for Bosniak classification.	

Prominent renal pelvis is only of concern after it goes above 1.0 cm.

For the third line, "No suspicious lesion, hydronephrosis or renal calculus is noted.", include all three points. So if there is a focal lesion, describe it, then the sentence after that should read, "No hydronephrosis or renal calculus is noted."
